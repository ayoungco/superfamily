# Mutunga (or “Te Mutunga”)

This chronicle is the only record of the events leading to the disbanding or disappearance of the Super Family. The following is merely a speculative account of the events leading up to then.

There was apparently an assassination attempt on Dr. Seinyor led by the Evil Brother executed with stealth and speed. No large assault was led. The Unlimiter, the classified artifact with divine power, was used to track the multiverse configuration the Super Family AC, their mobile space station, was operating in. The AC powers a rectangular portal that can target a specific 2 dimensional rectangular area in space in any multiverse using known factors. The more specific the configuration, the greater energy drawn. Overuse of the Unlimiter led to its energy signature propagating through space. By assembling a system of his own using cleverly small portals, the Evil Brother was able to use fast and stealthy operatives. By bypassing the Super Family’s preferred method of attack (gradually ramping up power and exponentially increasing the power of Hyper Supernova Beam), the Evil brother was able to accomplish this mission. The Unlimiter was targeted and disabled by the operatives, then Dr. Seinyor was attacked simultaneously. The assassination attempt was a success, but no body was found. The operatives dispersed quickly to different universes. 1 low level operative was detained .

The AC and its forces were kept in ready status for approximately six months, but the immense power requirements could not be sustained, even for a local force. Opening a portal was out of the question. Many or all of the Super Family had to disband and return to the places and times from which they had come. The residual Unlimiter energy was used to accomplish this. The technology the Super Family utilized for the Unlimiter was destroyed, and only Dr. Seinyor knew its engineering. The AC was parked in orbit around the Lego planet, perhaps permanently. A few members of the Super Family remain, in hiding, trying to figure out what happened to Dr. Seinyor. It is assumed they are set up on Lego Island, the Powerteam's original settlement. The AC is being converted into a residential space station and it’s droid army is in the process of being dismantled and reverse-engineered to recover the technology. While Dr. Seinyor’s refusal, or forgetfulness, to disclose details of the manufacture of such technology prevented the military from being maintained, it also prevented it from falling into the wrong hands.

The Heroes Legacy Preservation Society exists to investigate any and all sightings or communications from Dr. Seinyor. Publicly the organization denies any ongoing investigation of Dr. Seinyor’s whereabouts and publicly states that its belief is that he was killed in the operation. It states that its mission is to maintain and preserve the history of the Super Family, as well as track the operatives who killed him. The artifact powering the Unlimiter was never found or recovered. Enemy operatives were not seen escaping with it, however no conclusions have been drawn. Its energy and power cannot be detected, so it is believed to not be active. One remaining unexplained phenomenon is a “crack” or shear force of some description that the AC suffered at the moment Dr. Seinyor was defeated. A significant portion of the AC’s head, core, and right arm armaments were heavily damaged by this spatial shear, and the point in space where it happened orbits the star, like it has its own gravitational profile. None travel near this spatial anomaly because it’s properties are unknown. It is assumed extremely dangerous.

I was finally able to find paper and something to write with period let me describe my prior moments in detail as best I can recall them. I woke inside a dark vessel, and with struggle I was able to lift the lid of the vessel. I emerged into a subterranean crypt. As the candle light seared my vision, it took a while for it to adjust. When it cleared, I saw my skin was modeled, weak and Gray. The slightest touched caused it to crumble in places. Over the grave, I saw the name plaque: “B. Irascorus.” Memories flashed before me, but were gone in seconds. A full life, only work and despair and loneliness.

Then chaos. Monsters destroying my home, destroying, consuming. Skeletal forms and horrible amalgamations of flesh. Gnashing teeth, nauseating agony, then darkness. I had collapsed. I pulled myself off of the cold floor. I had become one of them, but how did I end up here? I remember a stunning shock of energy that woke me from death. Pierced my burial vessel, into my chest, left me gasping for air, if that’s what I needed.

More visions. This time with brilliant light and love. A lifetime of friendship, but also conflict. Great power… terminated by great fear. More confusion. “B. Irascorus,” or someone else? What is going on? I made an attempt to walk into the gloomy, evening lit forest ahead of me, but a few bats suddenly swarmed past me and I stumbled backward, falling down the stairs I just had painstakingly climbed. I landed with a thud and a sharp snapping noise.

My leg was twisted at a sickening angle and a few ribs poked out of my chest, but I felt no pain. Clearly I would have to make a conscious attempt to preserve myself, whoever I really was. This body wasn’t going to do it for me.
