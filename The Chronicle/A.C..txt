====== A.C. ======
Created Wednesday 25 June 2014

The former A.C. battle station was a colossal space station built in the image of an Armored Core about the radius of Earth in length. The "roof" of the Leg section consisted of an extremely large persistent one-way portal that would be "parked" above a current base of operations. For instance, the Super Family could travel to Mata Nui by "jumping" down into this portal. It is "projected" onto obsidian glass several meters thick and millions of kilometers in area, and for it to remain operational, the glass surface and composition must be kept pristine. The portal must be switched off if the AC engages in direct combat or has to defend itself.

The portal can more easily transport a large number of ships and materials

If the AC needs to transport itself, it must utilize its overboost to slide between universes at physics-defying speeds, and this uses incredible amounts of energy. All portals that the AC projects are one-directional, and in order for operatives to return to the AC, it must travel there "manually" (rarely) or send fast recovery ships which can "tow" sent troops and materials back to it.

Unfortunately the AC is notoriously slow on "impulse power" so it must make do with heavy armament and shielding to protect itself. Usual protocol dictates the AC remains out of combat if it must. However, the risk of the loss of the A.C. is so high that it is heavily equipped to deal with any kind of emergency.



The portal had no designation and was mostly a transparent tool for the Super Family's operations.
