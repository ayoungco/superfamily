====== The Super Family ======
Created Monday 21 April 2014

The Super Family is an interversal hero alliance with the mission of fighting evil. Previously chronicled through A.A. Network episodic movies dating from the creation of the House.

The Universal War
An interversal tear-storm in which many major conflicts in various universes occur all at the same time. Engineered by Makuta.

**Thoughts on the Narrative**

This is my own personal story so the only person I have to please is me. The story must give meaning to my own existence, an internal story to be a lone candle in the darkness. I live this world so long as I keep it living. 

I intend to mature it but keep previous revisions.
