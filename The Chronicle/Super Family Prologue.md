Super Family Prologue

The reality that man exists in is a conglomerate of his thoughts. Interpretation, understanding, imagination, creation, and comprehension all are the result of human cognition. That which can be thought of is just as real as

If you ask any number of men what reality is, you will most likely receive a mixed bag of answers. Some may say that what “is real” is that which exists physically in our own universe, that which we could potentially experience with our own human senses. I for one believe that which is real is what man can conceive or understand with his mind, whether he knows of its reality or not. If it can be thought, it can be real. And it is real. It is woven into the Fabric of Everything.
